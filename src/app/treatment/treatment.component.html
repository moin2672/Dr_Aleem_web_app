Polycystic Ovarian Disease

PCOD / PCOS is a very common condition affecting 5% to 10% of women in the age group 12- 45 years. It is a problem in which a woman's hormones are out of balance. It can cause problems with menstrual periods and make it difficult for her to conceive.
